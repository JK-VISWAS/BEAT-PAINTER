How to use Beat Painter
🖌️
Draw Music
Click and drag anywhere on the blank canvas to paint a musical note shape. The vertical position sets the pitch, and the width sets the duration of the shape.
🎹
Tools & Scales
Use the left panel (bottom sheet on mobile) to pick an instrument, adjust octave/volume, and select a musical scale to automatically lock your notes into key perfectly.
▶️
Playback Sequencer
Hit the Play button at the top. The playhead sweeps across the canvas from left to right, triggering the painted notes exactly where they sit on the timeline.
🔴
Record & Gallery
Hit the Record button to capture your masterpiece directly to an audio file. Find your saved audios and preset professional samples under the 🎵 Gallery panel.
